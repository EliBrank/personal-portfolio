ABOUT ME

My passion for digital art—illustration, animation, 3D modeling, graphic design—fueled a certain curiosity for software development. As I worked with tools like Blender and After Effects, I discovered their dedicated communities for scripting and began to understand the potential that coding could bring to my work.


I've now developed a solid foundation in various languages and frameworks, and it's a path I've now wished I had taken earlier. Programming scratches my itch for problem-solving while paving new avenues for creativity.
